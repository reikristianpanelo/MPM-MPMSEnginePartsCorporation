MPM/MPMS Engine Parts Corporation — Over 50 Years of Trusted Diesel Engine Parts Supply Quality Excellence

MPMS Engine Parts Corporation, formerly known as MPM Corporation, was established in 1975. For the past five decades, 50 years, the company has consistently surpassed expectations in supplying top-notch quality diesel engine parts to workshops, heavy equipment operators, and industrial clients across the Philippines. From humble beginnings to a well-established name, MPMS continues to stand for reliability, durability, and long-term customer trust.

01
Founded in 1975 — A Strong Legacy
Established in 1975 as MPM Corporation, the company began its operations serving local diesel engine users and repair workshops with dependable parts and service.
02
Growth and Rebranding to MPMS
As demand increased, the company expanded its product line and operations, eventually rebranding as MPMS Engine Parts Corporation to reflect its broader reach and improved service capabilities.
03
Commitment to Quality and Long-Term Trust
Today, MPMS continues its legacy of excellence, maintaining its commitment to supplying high-quality diesel engine parts and strong customer relationships built and strengthened over decades.
