History of FP Diesel:

FP Diesel has its roots in the FelPro brand. FelPro is the industry leader in under hood gasket solutions. In 1993, Fel-Pro launched a diesel parts line named FP Diesel. Subsequently, in 1997, Federal-Mogul acquired Fel-Pro and continued to build on the FP Diesel brand. In 2015, FP Diesel moved its distribution center and engineering team to Skokie, Illinois. In 2011, FP Diesel engine portfolio reached 25,000 SKUs.

Tenneco (FP Diesel’s parent company) is a global OE engine parts manufacturer, producing and delivering to OEMs, the OE spares market, and the aftermarket. We deliver FP Diesel engine parts globally for the vast majority of off-highway vehicle engines.
